🎮 Early Origins | 早期起源

1958 — American physicist William Higinbotham created Tennis for Two at Brookhaven National Laboratory. Displayed on an oscilloscope, it is widely recognized as the world's first interactive electronic game.

1958 — 美國物理學家 威廉·海吉諾比姆 (William Higinbotham) 在布魯克海文國家實驗室開發了《雙人網球 (Tennis for Two)》。使用示波器顯示畫面，被公認為史上第一個可互動的電子遊戲。

🏆 Birth of Pong | Pong 的誕生

1972 — Engineer Al Alcorn at Atari, tasked by founder Nolan Bushnell, designed Pong — a pixel-style table tennis game with simple block graphics. It became an instant sensation.

1972 — Atari 工程師 艾爾·艾爾科恩 (Al Alcorn)，接受創辦人 諾蘭·布什內爾 (Nolan Bushnell) 的任務，設計了《Pong》——一款以像素方塊與球組成的桌上型網球遊戲。一推出就造成轟動。

🔊 Where the Name Came From | 名稱由來

The name Pong mimics the sound of a ping-pong ball hitting a paddle. Some also trace it to the popular tabletop game Ping-Pong of the era. Atari was first to register it as a trademark.

Pong 這個名字來自於乒乓球撞擊球拍的聲音。另一說是取自當時流行的桌遊「Ping-Pong」命名。Atari 率先搶先登記了版權。

🌍 Impact & Legacy | 影響與意義

Pong brought video games into public spaces — restaurants, bars, and arcades. In the early 1980s, LCD handheld Pong machines became a childhood classic across Asia. The game established the paradigm of 2D graphics and paddle-style controls that shaped an entire generation of game design.

Pong 讓電子遊戲首次進入公共娛樂場所（餐廳、酒吧、遊樂場）。1980 年代初，LCD 掌上型 Pong 遊戲機風靡亞洲，成為許多人的童年回憶。這款遊戲奠定了2D 圖形、桿式控制器的遊戲典範，影響深遠。
